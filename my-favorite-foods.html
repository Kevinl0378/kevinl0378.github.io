My Favorite Foods



Pork Chive Dumplings

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Ground Pork
  • Eggs
  • Chinese Chives
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce

Pork chive dumplings require 2 hours to prepare and 10 minutes to cook. It usually costs around $25 for 40 dumplings.


Steamed Buns

Ingredients:

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Ground Pork
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Ground Bean Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steamed pork buns require 3 hours to prepare and 20 minutes to cook. It usually costs around $10 for 5 steamed buns.


Egg Custard Buns

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Melted Butter

Egg custard buns require 3 hours to prepare and 15 minutes to cook. It usually costs around $5 for 3 egg custard buns.
